Our Mission

The Longhorn Stream Team is a hands-on initiative that invites student volunteers and researchers to become stewards of their local water bodies. We collect and share water quality data to support a cleaner, healthier ecosystem in Central Texas.

Whether you're interested in field sampling, data science, or community outreach, there's a place for you on our team!

Current Goals

We are actively identifying ideal field sites near the UT Austin campus and pursuing funding to acquire the essential tools and equipment for our research.
